Welcome to **Valyria IoT**, a GitHub organization created to host the projects of the **Valyria group** for the [Programming the Internet of Things](https://www.unibo.it/en/study/course-units-transferable-skills-moocs/course-unit-catalogue/course-unit/2025/541868) course at the [University of Bologna](https://www.unibo.it/en).

## About

This organization brings together the work developed by the Valyria group as part of the **Programming the Internet of Things** course at the University of Bologna. Our projects explore IoT concepts, embedded systems, and connected device programming.
